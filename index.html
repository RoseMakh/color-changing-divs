Move your mouse, pointer, or finger over the large box to change the colors of the squares inside it.

Use the Hue, Saturation, and Lightness selections to change those aspects of the squares you interact with.

Use the Small, Medium, and Large selections to change the size and number of squares in the large box.

Hit the Reset the Colors button to reset all the squares to white.

Click the Inner button to enter Inner Select mode. This mode lets you move your mouse over the squares without changing their colors. Click any square inside the large box, and you'll be able to change their colors again. Click any square again to keep the colors from changing. This mode is great for getting the squares to the exact colors you want. Click the Inner button again to exit Inner Selection mode.

Click the List button to enter List Selection mode. In this mode, the squares in the large box can't be interacted with. This mode provides a list of all the colors currently in the big square. Click the X button on any color in the list to delete it. Click the List button again to exit List mode. The list of colors will automatically disappear.

Click the Print button to print large box with its current colored squares. In List mode, the list of colors will print below the large box.
